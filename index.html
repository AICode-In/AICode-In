HOW DOES IT WORK?

AI Code-in is a 7 weeks long contest, where students work with mentors and organisations on AI-related (CV, NLP) tasks. Students will receive professional mentorship and be evaluated on their submission quality & quantity.

STUDENTS

Students with interest to avail of this opportunity, to give a step up to their talent need to apply at student-code-in and select the project on which they need to make headway. According to their motive, they will be assigned a mentor who will give their utmost devotion and dedication.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can sign up as a mentor?

    Anyone who is experienced in Machine Learning and enthusiastic about AI education!

  • Who can sign up as a student?

    Any middle school or high school student with a passion for AI and open source!

  • What projects will there be?

    We will review all project proposals and select the most suitable! Accepted projects will be announced on Sep 5 2020.

  • Who can submit project proposals?

    Anyone! Feel free to share your ideas!

  • How will students, mentors and projects be matched?

    Organzations or projects can either provide their own mentors or request mentors from us. Students will choose one project they are most interested in and work on that project's tasks.

  • How will participants working on the same projects communicate with each other?

    Project groups can communicate with each other in any way they want. We will provide you with a project Slack channel at the beginning.

  • Must projects be hosted on Github?

    Yes. Github is the leading platform for open-source development and is easy to use. It is beneficial to gain experience with Github if you want to work on open-source projects in the future!

  • How are winners selected?

    At the end of the competition, each organization or project's mentors will discuss and nominate 1 student to be the winner of the organization or project based on the quality and quantity of tasks completed.

  • What will participants receive after the conclusion of AI Code-In?

    All successful participants will receive a digital certificate of participation. Winners will receive a digital ecrtificate and monetary rewards. (Exact amount to be announced)

  • Can we continue developing our project after the competition ends?

    Definitely! You should communicate with your project mentor for more information.

  • Why AI?

    AI has accomplished tasks we have never believed computers could do. It makes phone calls for you, it composes art and music, it will drive cars on our roads one day. AI is changing our world for good, and you can be the catalyst in bringing out AI's potentials.

  • How can I get started?
    We will invite researchers and industry professionals to give guest lectures during the competition period. We ourselves will be rolling out elementary AI tutorial videos over youtube. Stay tuned!
